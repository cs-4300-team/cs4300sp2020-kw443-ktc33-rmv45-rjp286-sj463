Our goal is to provide a personalized party playlist to a group of people based on their personal playlists. This app would allow users to link their Spotify playlists to produce one playlist for everyone based on their music preferences. The target audience would be people who listen to music and want an easy way to create a playlist that fits everyone's taste of music. This would be unique because we don't think there is a service which returns a playlist for everyone based on their own music preferences. The data would be the set of playlists retrieved from Spotify and sets of comments from Reddit pages about different songs. It will search for songs in common with other users from our scraped playlists as well as new songs recommended for everyone after analyzing similarities between Reddit user comments on the song pages. It will also look at other metrics such as genre, artists, and audio features (e.g. tempo, loudness, energy, etc) in developing a shared playlist.
